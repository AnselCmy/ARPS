报告题目：大规模MIMO系统导频污染消除
《Pilot Contamination Free Massive MIMO》
 报告时间：2017年6月14日（星期三）上午9:30
地点：信息工程学院205多媒体教室
报告人简介：
陈生，英国皇家工程学院院士，IEEE会士（FIEEE)， IET会士（FIET），现为英国南安普敦大学(University of Southampton)电子与计算机科学学院教授。陈生教授在自适应信号处理以及非线性系统建模、控制和无线通信、智能学习系统等多个研究领域有着若干开创性研究成果，在国际高水平学术期刊上发表500余篇论文，2004年陈生荣膺ISI全球工程类高被引学者。目前，美国科学信息协会(ISI)统计陈生教授论文他引次数超过10300次，Google学术统计其论文引用超过23000次。
报告摘要：
MIMO promises a wonderland for wireless communications. To reach this promised wonderland requires accurate MIMO channel state information (CSI). Massive MIMO promises a ‘massive’ wonderland for wireless communications, but also imposing a massive problem of acquisition of CSI, commonly referred to as pilot contamination (PC). It is a stumbling block preventing us from reaching massive MIMO promised wonderland.
The existing state-of-the-arts are ineffective and impractical, either requiring huge amount of user information acquisition and information exchange between cells or imposing excessive training overhead.
In this talk, we will show how to solve this challenging PC problem. It proves the best solution is also the simplest one.
