Abstract：
Learning-based approaches to information problems such as document ranking are data hungry: they need considerable amounts of training data to achieve a good performance. Over the years, many approaches have been put forward to automatically or semi-automatically generate training material for ranking problems. In the talk I will survey recent advances and outline future directions, especially in view of personal search assistants and of neural network-based approaches to document retrieval.
