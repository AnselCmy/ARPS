Abstract：
In the beginning, the presentation will illustrate how to develop 2D/3D multi-scale and multi-resolution agent based cancer models (MMABM) for Glioma cancer research as well as the advantages of MMABM. And then, it will describe how to use latest graphic processing units (GPU) to increase the performance of MMABM. Next, this presentation will briefly introduce my upcoming research plan to apply parallel MMABM for multi-myeloma cancer. Finally, it will show how to build up a 3D continuum skin cancer model to forecast the laser power and treatment time for the skin cancer hyperthermia treatment.
