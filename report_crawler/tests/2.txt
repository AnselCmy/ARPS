
报告题目：MIMO Radar and Probing Waveform Synthesis
报 告 人：李健 美国佛罗里达大学/中国科学技术大学 教授
报告时间：2017年7月10日（星期一）上午10:30——11:30
报告地点：郑州大学新校区信息工程学院院办公楼205多媒体教室
座谈时间：2017年7月10日（星期一）下午14:30——16:00
座谈地点：郑州大学新校区信息工程学院工科园109会议
目前李健教授担任IEEE信号处理学会的传感器阵列和多通道技术委员会成员。李健教授是2005年度IEEE航天与电子系统学报上最佳论文奖“巴里-卡尔顿奖”获奖论文合作者，和2013年度IEEE信号处理学会信号处理期刊最佳论文奖论文合作者。室报告摘要：Unlike a standard phased-array radar, MIMO radar can transmit via its antennas multiple probing signals that may be correlated or uncorrelated with each other. We provide herein an overview of some recent results on MIMO radar with colocated antennas, showing that this waveform diversity enables the MIMO radar superiority in several fundamental aspects. Moreover, probing waveform synthesis for MIMO radar is also discussed. We will provide a review of recent novel, cyclic approaches to single and multiple probing waveform designs. Both aperiodic and periodic correlations will be considered. We show that by making use of fast Fourier transforms (FFTs), we can now efficiently design sequences that were previously impossible to synthesize. Furthermore, we will present the computationally efficient SHAPE algorithm for shaping the spectrum of probing sequences to accommodate spectrum sharing.李健教授个人简介：Jian Li received the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from The Ohio State University, Columbus, in 1987 and 1991, respectively.
