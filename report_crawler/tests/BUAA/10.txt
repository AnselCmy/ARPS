Title：
Scientific Publishing from the Inside Out


Time：
2016年7月4日10:00——11:30


Address：
北京航空航天大学新主楼第二报告厅


Speaker：
《Science》副主编Brent Grocholski


Organizer：
北京航空航天大学计算机学院

Biography：
Brent Grocholski is a physical science associate editor at Science. He handles and edits manuscripts primarily in geology, geophysics, geochemistry, and materials science. Brent’s research background is in experimental high pressure physics. Before starting as an editor in 2014, he was a Peter Buck fellow at the National Museum of Natural History, Smithsonian Institution where he researched the Earth’s rocky mantle. He received his Ph. D. in Earth and Planetary Science at the University of California-Berkeley and postdoctoral work at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.


Abstract：
有效的科学交流，需要良好的写作水平，也需要对出版过程的了解。特邀请《Science（科学）》副主编Brent Grocholski为我校师生做“Scientific Publishing from the Inside Out”讲座。在此次讲座中，我们将从《Science》期刊编辑的角度，讨论宽泛的主题。包括写作的各个方面、同行评审和出版过程等等。使你深入了解如何在例如《Science》这样高知名度的期刊发表文章。
